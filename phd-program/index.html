Programme Details

Ph.D or Doctor of Philosophy is a doctorate degree with focus on academics & research. In general, Ph.D course duration is 3 years & a student can complete it within a maximum time span of 5 years. The basic Ph.D eligibility criteria is to have a masters degree with a minimum aggregate of 55% marks. There’s a 5% relaxation for all those candidates too who belong to SC/ST/OBCs and differently abled categories.

Ph.D admission is done on the basis of the score of UGC NET/CSIR UGC NET or if you wish to persue Ph.D in Engineering and Technology then yau need to possess a GATE score. This doctorate level degree can be pursued in a variety of specializatians that fall under Engineering, Management, Commerce, Arts, Humanities and a like Before 2017, Doctor of Philosaphy was pursued in a distance education made, but now as per UGC, it can be pursued only in full-time or part-time mode.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Post Graduation (PG / Masters) in any relevant subject or any equivalent professional degree from a recognized institution with minimum 55% marks or 5.5 CGPA (on a 10 point scale)
  • NET/ JRF/ GATE/ SLET/ SET/ MPhil qualified candidates and Teachers Fellow are exempted from the written examination.
    (A teaching fellow is an individual at a higher education institution, including universities, whose role involves teaching and potentially pedagogic research)
  • CA/ CS/ ICWA qualification be considered equivalent to the PG course for admission to the Ph.D. programme of university.
