Programme Details

B.Sc. in AME (Aircraft Maintenance Engineering) is a technical training course (3 years long) that will help you acquire an aircraft maintenance engineer license (issued by the DGCA).

B.Sc. in Aircraft Maintenance Engineering is an undergraduate programme related to the Aircraft Maintenance Engineering. In this course, the student will learn about the maintenance, repair, and solving the problems of an aircraft. AME is responsible for keeping the aircraft in airworthy condition. This degree deal with the safety and security of aircraft and its passengers.

B.Sc. in Aircraft Maintenance Engineering comprises of theoretical and practical sessions by the well qualified and experienced trainers. The practical session allows the student to learn the concept in a better way.

Programme Structure

B.Sc. in AME (Aircraft Maintenance Engineering) is a undergraduate programme of three years duration. Each year is divided into two semesters i.e. total 6 semesters. The B.Sc. AME syllabus consists of the subjects that help the student to maintain, repair, and troubleshoot the issue faced in an aircraft.
